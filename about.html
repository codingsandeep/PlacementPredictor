About Our Model

Our placement prediction model uses machine learning algorithms to analyze student data and predict placement outcomes with high accuracy.

The model considers various factors such as CGPA, projects, skills, and more to provide personalized predictions. Built using Random Forest and XGBoost algorithms, our model has been trained on data from 10,000 students with diverse academic backgrounds.
